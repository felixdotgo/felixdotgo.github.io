Hi, I'm **Felix** — a software engineer who writes about technology and the occasional life detour.

I spend most of my time building backend systems, mostly in Go. I care about tools that stay out of your way and codebases that are honest about what they do. I'm skeptical of complexity and fond of deleting things.

Outside of work, I co-built [QueryBox](https://github.com/felixdotgo/querybox) — a lightweight, plugin-based database GUI for people who just want to run queries and look at data without paying a cognitive tax for features they'll never use.

I also write here. Posts tend to be short: a fix that took too long to find, a setup worth sharing, or a thought that needed somewhere to land.
